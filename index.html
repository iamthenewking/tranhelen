Bio

I am the Founder of Jupiter, a Physique Competitor, Product Designer, and Design Educator. I was born and raised in Toronto and am proud to call myself a 7-Continent citizen.

I have spent 12 years designing and creating things, mostly software. From 2013-2017 I was at Shopify as it scaled from 300 to 3000+ employees. I am most proud of working on the foundations of sales channels (now their App Platform) and helping to build out a Product Design team of 30 as a Design Lead.

In Canada my design work is used by a few large grocery chains and by mostly everyone buying a movie ticket online. Worldwide, my work has impacted 500,000+ merchants, the developers building for them, and consumers buying through various social media networks.

Currently I am building a company which creates software for beauty professionals at Jupiter and teach Design methods and skills to others.

In my spare time I do a lot of other things and write about roughly half of them on the internet.
