Helen Tran

Designer / Entrepreneur

I’m a Designer who builds digital products. I enjoy growing businesses with a deliberate and curious mindset.

Most recently, I was at Shopify for four years, the last two of which were spent as a Design Lead building out a team of 30 designers. These days I'm working on a new software product of my own, still focused on helping entrepreneurs become more successful.

I have been and continue to teach design, write articles about design and leadership, and occasionally make myself available for interviews and podcasts. Design is a wonderful viewpoint from which to see the world and I aim to make that viewpoint accessible.

For fun, I write and compete with myself. I like to thru-hike long trails (longest so far was the Kungsleden for 430km) and compete as an amateur fitness athlete. I live to learn and tend to fully commit myself to new interests out of curiousity.

My life is shared with two succulents named Dante and Sedona. We live in Toronto and I never feel bad about saying it’s objectively the best city in the world. Fire emoji.
